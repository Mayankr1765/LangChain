Company Name: LangChain Community
Document: HR Policy Handbook
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Leave Policy:
- Casual Leave: Employees are entitled to 12 days of casual leave per year.
- Sick Leave: Employees can avail up to 8 days of paid sick leave annually.
- Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Paternity Leave: Male employees can take 7 days of paid paternity leave.
- Leave Carry Forward: Up to 6 unused casual leaves can be carried forward to the next calendar year.

2. Work Hours & Attendance:
- Office Hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Break Time: 1-hour lunch break between 1:00 PM and 2:00 PM.
- Attendance: Employees must clock in by 9:30 AM. Late entries beyond 3 times a month require approval.

3. Remote Work Policy:
- Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 8 days per month with manager approval.
- Work-from-home requests should be submitted 24 hours in advance.

4. Code of Conduct:
- Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, punctuality, and decorum at all times.
- Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct will lead to disciplinary action.

5. Employee Benefits:
- Health Insurance: All employees and their immediate family are covered under group health insurance.
- Provident Fund: Company contributes 12% of basic salary to employee PF accounts.
- Performance Bonus: Based on individual and company performance, bonuses are paid annually.
